## Slide 2 — The problem

### Finding reliable home help in Lusaka is still informal

- WhatsApp groups, word of mouth, no-shows
- Hard to know who is verified or available
- Busy households waste time coordinating
- Good workers lack a steady stream of fair jobs

**Speaker notes:**  
"Most families still find help through referrals and random numbers. There's no standard booking, pricing, or accountability. Workers who are serious also struggle to find consistent work."

|-----------|---------|----------|

| Pick date, time & scope | Get booking requests | Handles scheduling rules |
