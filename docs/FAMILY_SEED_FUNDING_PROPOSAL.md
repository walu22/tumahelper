## 1. Executive summary

**TumaHelper** connects households in Lusaka with verified home-help workers — for cleaning, nannies, housekeeping, cooking, laundry, garden work, and handyman jobs.

The product is **already built** as a working MVP: website, booking flows, worker profiles, scheduling rules, payment proof workflow, terms & privacy, and automated tests. This is not funding an idea on paper — it is funding **launch, legal setup, and reliable operations**.

I am requesting **ZMW 52,000** (approximately **USD 1,900**) as one-time family seed funding to cover:

1. **MacBook Air** — primary development and business machine  
2. **Company registration & compliance** — PACRA, trading licence, business banking setup  
3. **App and platform fees** — domain, hosting, SMS, developer accounts, first-year tools  

In return, I commit to a **90-day launch plan**, **monthly 1-page progress reports**, and a **shared expense tracker with receipts**.

## 2. What TumaHelper is (plain language)

| Who | What they get |
|-----|----------------|
| **Households** | Book trusted help online by service, area, date, and time |
| **Workers** | Profile, verification, booking requests, earnings visibility |

**Services live today in the product:**

## 6. Revenue model & payback scenario

*Based on guide prices already in the TumaHelper product (typical home clean ≈ ZMW 350–550; nanny/day services higher).*

### How TumaHelper earns

| Metric | Example |
|--------|---------|
| Average booking value | ZMW 450 |
| Platform fee (10%) | ZMW 45 per completed booking |
| Worker earnings (90%) | ZMW 405 |

### Conservative scenarios (platform revenue only)

| Monthly completed bookings | Avg booking (ZMW) | Gross booking volume | TumaHelper fee (10%) | Annual platform revenue |
|---------------------------|-------------------|----------------------|----------------------|-------------------------|
| 25 | 450 | 11,250 | **1,125** | 13,500 |
| 50 | 450 | 22,500 | **2,250** | 27,000 |
| 100 | 450 | 45,000 | **4,500** | 54,000 |
| 150 | 500 | 75,000 | **7,500** | 90,000 |

### Simple payback math (ZMW 52,000 seed)

| Scenario | Monthly platform revenue | Months to recover seed* |
|----------|-------------------------|-------------------------|
| Slow start (25 bookings/mo) | ZMW 1,125 | ~46 months |
| Target (50 bookings/mo) | ZMW 2,250 | ~23 months |
| Growth (100 bookings/mo) | ZMW 4,500 | ~12 months |

\* *Before operating costs (SMS, hosting, marketing). Assumes all bookings complete and fee is collected. Real payback depends on execution.*

### What “success” looks like in Year 1

- **Break-even on monthly costs** (~ZMW 2,000–4,000/mo for hosting, SMS, marketing): roughly **45–90 completed bookings/month**
- **Meaningful side income** (ZMW 5,000+/mo platform revenue): **110+ bookings/month**
- **Full-time viable** (ZMW 15,000+/mo platform revenue): **330+ bookings/month** — requires team, marketing, and strong worker supply

*These are targets, not guarantees. Included so you can see how the business could eventually return value.*

**Milestone:** Bookings happening weekly without hand-holding every single one.
